# Mission

The Kanaka PAC website exists to keep PAC families **informed, engaged, and empowered**.

It is the single place where parents, guardians, and community members can find everything they need to participate in school life: upcoming events, meeting minutes, announcements, policies, and how to get in touch.

## Core purpose

- **Inform** — publish timely announcements, event details, and meeting records so every family stays in the loop.
- **Engage** — make it easy to subscribe to email updates and show up to what matters.
- **Empower** — surface the PAC's governance documents and decisions so the community can meaningfully participate.

## Who it serves

| Audience | Need |
|---|---|
| Parents & guardians | Know what's happening, opt into updates |
| PAC admin/executive | Publish and manage content without technical help |
| New families | Understand the PAC's mission, structure, and how to get involved |

## What success looks like

A parent who hasn't visited the site before can — within two minutes — find the next event, read the latest announcement, and subscribe to email updates. An admin can publish new content or send an email blast in under five minutes.

## Scope boundaries

The site handles **communication and transparency**. It does not handle fundraising transactions, school-system integrations, or real-time messaging. Keep features tightly scoped to the core purpose above; complexity should serve families, not the other way around.
